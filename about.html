Honest to Goodness

Honest Health is a family owned and operated outdoor farm nestled in the foothills of Northern California. We start our plants from seeds. And we provide high quality organic nourishment to our beloved plants, without the use of pesticides. We are dedicated to researching and breeding plants that produce the highest medicinal concentration of CBD and THC. We specialize in the production of cannabis with high CBD concentration, and are working toward launching a line of CBD edibles and topical creams.
